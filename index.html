LASA Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

Welcome to LASAFlavor ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Summary: LASAFlavor is a food delivery app that connects customers with local Filipino restaurants. We collect only the information necessary to process your orders and provide our services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name: Your first and last name
  • Email Address: Used for account authentication and order confirmations
  • Phone Number: Used for delivery coordination
  • Delivery Address: Where you want your orders delivered

Order Information

When you place an order, we collect:

  • Items ordered and quantities
  • Delivery preferences and instructions
  • Order history

Payment Information

Payment processing is handled securely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card numbers, CVV, or full payment details on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy can be found at stripe.com/privacy.

Device Information

We may collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Push notification tokens (for order updates)
  • App usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your food orders
  • Communicate with you about your orders
  • Send push notifications about order status updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our app and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Restaurants: Your name, order details, and delivery address are shared with restaurants to fulfill your orders.
  • With Delivery Personnel: Your name, phone number, and delivery address are shared to complete deliveries.
  • With Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services including:
    • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and analytics
    • Stripe: For secure payment processing
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Firebase Authentication for secure account access
  • PCI-compliant payment processing through Stripe
  • Regular security reviews and updates

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Order history is retained for record-keeping and customer service purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications in your device settings

7. Children's Privacy

LASAFlavor is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to request deletion of your data.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
